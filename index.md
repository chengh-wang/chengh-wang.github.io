I am a final-year Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, where I work with Prof. Alireza Ramezani. My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and hardware — particularly policy design that enables polyarticular mechanisms to achieve agile physical behavior. My long-term goal is to develop agile, intelligent, and robust controls that ultimately achieve and surpass human-level intelligence.
